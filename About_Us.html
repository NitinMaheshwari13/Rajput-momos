ABOUT THE FOUNDER


What started almost a back in the bylanes of C-Scheme, Jaipur is now spread across the country and seeing 20+ stores operational of Rajput Momos does make Rajupt's a proud entrepreneur. Starting from a humble background and with great help from his Family , being a same industry experience, Rajput started this restaurant in 2022. With his strong business acumen & his passion for serving good food he is an established name in the restaurant industry, known for his sharp marketing skills & understanding the customer requirements to the hilt.




BRAND OVERVIEW


Classic dishes are a staple, but sometimes you need to take your taste buds on an adventure. Rajput momos is the fastest growing Pan Asian Restaurant specializing in Modern Chinese and Continental fusion cuisine. Our food is prepared fresh daily in our kitchen with the highest regards to quality. We use In-house products and adjust our menu according to what is in the season to ensure our food is fresh and authentic. We are also conscious of the amounts of sugar, salt, and fats that we use in our cooking. We are the masters of Home delivery and have a renowned presence on all delivery aggregators and deliver tasty food, neatly packed all across through our own app & website. Our team is passionate and innovative which is reflected in our menu where traditional recipes are given a modern twist. Each recipe has been crafted with great care and carefully selected by our team to bring you vibrant and intense flavors to make you feel like you’ve been transported to China.
