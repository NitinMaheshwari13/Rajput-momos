Rajput Momos



Classic dishes are a staple, but sometimes you need to take your taste buds on an adventure. Rajput momos is a home delivery service specialising in Modern Chinese and Continental fusion cuisine. Our food is prepared fresh daily in our kitchen with the highest regards to quality. We use In house products and adjust our menu according to what is in season to ensure our food is fresh and authentic. We are also conscious of the amounts of sugar, salt and fats that we use in our cooking. Our team is passionate and innovative which is reflected in our menu where traditional recipes are given a modern twist. Each recipe has been crafted with great care and carefully selected by our team to bring you vibrant and intense flavours to make you feel like you've been transported to China.
